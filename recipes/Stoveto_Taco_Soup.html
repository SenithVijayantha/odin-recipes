Stovetop Taco Soup Recipe

This recipe is easy to adapt to a slow cooker:

Brown the ground chuck, onions, and garlic as instructed in
the recipe.

Transfer the beef mixture to a 6-quart slow cooker. Add all of
the soup ingredients except for the cheese and cilantro.

Cook the soup on low for 6 to 7 hours. Stir in the cheese and
cilantro just before serving.
